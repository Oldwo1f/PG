Nouvelle Erre Hair Salon - 20 Modern Before/After Facebook Templates

Based on your request for more modern templates inspired by the uploaded images, I've expanded the collection to 20 varied templates. These draw inspiration from the tropical, organic vibes in the images (e.g., swirling spirals, braided elements, bamboo, palm leaves, gold accents, and abstract shapes in turquoise/mint palettes). I've kept them minimalistic and flat as per the original brief: no text, no busy collages, unified compositions, with the central swirling spiral of braided bamboo (or subtle variations like intertwined leaves or abstract braids) in the upper half, and the lower half blank for adding before/after photos, text, or logos. Variations include different gradient directions, color emphases, slight modern twists (e.g., abstract leaf accents or spiral shapes inspired by the images), while maintaining a serene, organic tropical feel. All are square (1:1) for Facebook. Slight modernizations include softer gradients and subtle textures for a contemporary look.
